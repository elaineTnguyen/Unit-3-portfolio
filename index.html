Social Media Pressures

We decided to choose this topic because this topic is not discussed often. We wanted to educate all audiences on this issue and how minor comments could make a huge impact on people of all ages. This can positively benefit the community by spreading awareness and preventing bad activity through social media. We plan on accomplishing these goals by including data along with solutions we could all take part in.
